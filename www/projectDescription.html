Inspiration

When we first heard about iGEM, we also heard about the wonders of synthetic biology, and were more than inspired by the projects we read about. Learning about all the things that had been done only reaffirmed the notion that not even the sky was our limit. So what better idea than to use all these incredible tools to solve a real world issue in such a way that we could notice an impact in our own community?

Our team is comprised of people who have lived most of their lives in the state of Chihuahua. Knowing our home and our people for so many years and in so many different aspects, we wanted to do something that would truly influence our community. We wanted to create a meaningful change in our surroundings. With this as our main focus, we launched into our brainstorming sessions. A topic that resurfaced throughout all the wild and countless ideas we considered, was agriculture.

For our country, agriculture is one of the major economic activities. Besides being a leading agent in the job market, it fulfills the nutritional needs of millions of citizens, preserves natural environments, stimulates progress by improving the quality of life in rural areas, and has also been ingrained in our culture and traditions since the very beginning. The Mexican countryside counts itself among the 12 economies with the highest agricultural production on the world; employing 5.5 million people and generating around 27 billion dollars annually. 1

Even before being aware of these impressive statistics, we already knew agriculture had a considerable impact in our country and state. Before being part of iGEM Tec-Chihuahua 2019, some of our team members had the chance to meet and work with people immersed in this area, others had developed small research projects in our school regarding agricultural issues, but all of us had some sort of experience with agriculture and its importance for the Mexican people.

The interest, curiosity, and familiarity with this topic remained a constant throughout the first weeks we spent searching for that special cause we would focus our project on. Once we realized that, we began exploring what issues influenced this area the most so we could try and solve them.
